The Open Computing Facility (OCF) is an all-volunteer student organization dedicated to free computing for all University of California at Berkeley students, student groups, faculty, and staff.

  • Free shell account (SSH)
  • Free disk & web space (2 GB)
  • Free email (username@OCF.Berkeley.EDU)
  • Free printing (250 pages per semester)
  • Spiffy computer lab in Eshleman Hall, ground floor
  • and more...

The OCF provides virtual hosting (yourgroup.Berkeley.EDU) to numerous student groups on campus.
