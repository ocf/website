Open Computing Facility Web Badges for Virtual Hosts

Logos for groups with virtual hosting:

These logos are for organizations that have requested virtual hosting with the OCF, or for anyone else that wants to use them. You can either use the images hosted on our site, or you can download them and place them under your own. You're welcome to design your own too, but please contact the Site Manager: sm@ocf.berkeley.edu if you do so.

About these logos:

The "metal" and "lighter" images were the original logos created by ceugene in 2000.
"binnov" was contributed by the student group "Berkeley Innovation" in 2003.
"ocf-hosting-flag-wave" was contributed by the student group "Society for Creative Anachronism" in 2005.
The "berknow" logo was created by AH Design Net for the student group Berkeley NOW in 2006.
The "silver", "mini", "platinum" edition logos were original logos created by gordeon in 2007.
The "blue" logo is based off the "silver" logo and was contributed by the student group Ensorcelled in 2008.
