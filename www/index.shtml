The Open Computing Facility (OCF) is an all-volunteer student organization dedicated to free computing for all University of California at Berkeley students, faculty, and staff. Among our services, we offer:

  • Free shell account (SSH)
  • Free disk & web space (2 GB)
  • Free printing (250 pages/semester)
  • Spiffy computer lab in 6A Hearst Gym
  • and more...

The OCF offers hosting to numerous student groups on campus. Among the services offered to groups, we offer:

  • Free web hosting for official groups and registered student groups
  • Virtual hosting (yourgroup.Berkeley.EDU)
  • Same privileges as individual accounts
  • and more...
