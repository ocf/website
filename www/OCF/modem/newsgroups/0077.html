Lot of arguing about the campus modems; it seems to me that the
one hour idle timeout is extremeley generous. Why don't we shorten
that up a whole lot? I mean, is it really okay for someone to only
use their modem connection once per hour? Once every 15 minutes
sounds more reasonable to me.

I realize that shortening the idle timeouts may cause some people
to be disconnected inconveniently, but then, perhaps it'd be that
much easier to dial in. A *lot* less inconvenient than the 2 hour
hard limit, I'll say that! I mean, should I really have to get an
ISP just so I can download IE4.0? (Hey, I just want to LOOK at it,
okay? =)
