or
1) Download that 10MB file to a /tmp on a unix account (I believe you have
	atleast one, Indi). This should be faster than 2hours (since the
	unix host will undoubtedly have a faster connection).
2) Use a splitter program (various ones are available on the net) to split
	the 10mb fie into smaller chunks.
3) Download the smaller chunks, perhaps dialing in more than once to
	do so.
4) Put all the chunks together on your machine.
