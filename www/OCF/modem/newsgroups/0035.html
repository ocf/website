The modem scarcity, and the 2-hr limit imposed as an attempt to
deal with it make the downloading of large files difficult or impossible.

Meanwhile, IS&T is considering eliminating BIK distribution. Users would
instead be expected to download any applications not included with their
O.S.

I suggest that the 2-hr limit makes the distribution of an updated BIK on
portable media more important than ever.
