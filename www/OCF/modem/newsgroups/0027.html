)How do you intend that the university provide access to web sites
)restricted to within Berkeley domain names, such as (as have been
)mentioned) Encylcopedia Britannica and the Depot and Cobweb software

The campus could easily run a proxy server for such services, or could
contract out a service on the campus network to an outside vendor.  But the
faculty modems were paid for by a grant specifically for faculty modem
access, so I don't think it would be possible to pull the plug on them.
I essentially agree with Ryan, however, that the idea of a campus modem
bank being an entitlement is nonsensical.  Someone's gotta pay for it,
and it seems reasonable that it should be the people using it.
