Cliff,

Is this other university UCLA?  I have only been here a few months but I
did notice that they have both a warhol-like modem pool (20-min timeout)
and a general modem pool with a 4-hour timeout.  Perhaps they could be
contacted to determine the effectiveness of the 4-hour timeout.

See:  

	http://www.cns.ucla.edu/  

for plots of modem usage, etc.

They have some nice pie charts that show the percentage of connections
(see http://www.cns.ucla.edu/images/pie_public_UCD2_9.gif for an example)

	1) less than 20 min
	2) between 20 min and 1 hour
	3) between 1 hour and 2 hours
	4) more than 2 hours

Interestingly enough, only 4.4% of the connections are longer than 2 hours...
