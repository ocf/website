Policies governing use of the OCF Computers

All users of the Open Computing Facility computer systems have signed an form agreeing to follow the policies linked to below, including OCF policies as set by the OCF Board of Directors, and any and all applicable campus and UC system policies, and the laws of the State of California and the United States.

  • OCF Policies
  • U. C. Berkeley Policies and Guidelines for Use of Campus Computing and Network Resources
  • University of California Electronic Mail Policy
  • University of California Policies Applying to Campus Activities, Organizations, and Students

To report a violation of one of the above policies by an OCF user, you can contact us at <abuse@ocf.berkeley.edu>.
