Policy on Virtual Hosting

As approved by the Board of Directors on x/x/xxxx. The Open Computing Facility can provide Domain name hosting for sites under the Berkeley.EDU and OCF.Berkeley.EDU domains. (e.g. Wonderworks.Berkeley.EDU). You mean I can get Stanfurd-sucks.Berkeley.EDU?
Nope. Only certain entities are eligible for this service. At present, individuals are not eligible for custom domain names, but SAS registered student groups are. Other campus recognized groups may be eligible. The Buck Stops Where? The OCF Board of Directors reserves the right to offer this service on a case-by-case basis.
